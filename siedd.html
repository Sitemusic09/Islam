Usama Syed (Arabic: أسامة سيد; born 5 May 1994), better known by his stage name Siedd, is a Pakistani-Canadian singer-songwriter from Thunder Bay who signed with Makkah Records in 2016. He is a graduate of mechanical engineering from Lakehead University. In 2021, he showcased the Thunder Bay Community Auditorium in his music video called ‘Remember Me’.
